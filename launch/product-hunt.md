## Tagline (60 chars max)
Typing practice that learns your weaknesses and adapts to you

## Description
EchoType is a typing trainer that tracks your exact error patterns — which keys you confuse, which bigrams slow you down, which words trip you up — and generates text specifically designed to challenge your weak spots.

Unlike typing tests that just measure your speed, EchoType is a coach that makes you better. Every session builds a deeper model of your typing "DNA", and every round gets smarter about what you need to practice.

**Key features:**
- Adaptive text generation from 2000+ real English sentences
- Typing DNA fingerprint — a unique visualization of your error patterns
- Flow Score — a single metric combining speed, accuracy, and consistency
- Ghost Race mode — compete against your own past performance
- Targeted Drill mode — sentences loaded with your weakest patterns
- Daily Challenge — same text for everyone, compare with friends
- Custom Text — paste your own code, articles, or emails to practice on

**Built for:**
- Programmers who want faster, more accurate code typing
- Remote workers writing emails and docs all day
- Students preparing for typing-heavy exams
- Anyone who types for a living and wants to get better, not just faster

## Maker Comment
I was frustrated that MonkeyType tells me I type at 65 WPM but never helps me improve. I built EchoType to answer the question: "I know my speed — now how do I actually get better?"

The key insight is that everyone has a unique error fingerprint. Maybe you always fumble "th" → "ht", or your right pinky is slow on "p". EchoType finds these patterns and builds practice text around them.

It's free, runs in the browser, no account needed. Your data stays in your browser (localStorage) — no server, no tracking.
